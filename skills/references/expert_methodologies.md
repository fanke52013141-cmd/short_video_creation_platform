# Expert Methodologies

只在执行对应 Skill、需要判断依据时读取相关章节。

## Story development

- John Truby: use a connected dramatic chain rather than isolated beats; relate weakness/need, desire, opponent, plan, battle, self-revelation, and new equilibrium.
- Andrew Stanton: make the audience care, provide a unifying promise, and give clues that let the audience infer meaning instead of explaining everything.
- Sources: https://truby.com/anatomy-of-story-audio-class/ and https://www.ted.com/talks/andrew_stanton_the_clues_to_a_great_story

## Visual direction

- Bruce Block: design space, line, shape, tone, color, movement, and rhythm; control visual intensity through contrast and affinity; align visual progression with story progression.
- Source: https://www.routledge.com/The-Visual-Story-Creating-the-Visual-Structure-of-Film-TV-and-Digital/Block/p/book/9781138014152

## Character reference

- Animation Mentor: use character sheets to keep characters on-model; define readable identity anchors and review from multiple useful angles.
- Source: https://www.animationmentor.com/blog/7-animation-tips-to-make-your-work-rock/

## Continuity

- ScreenSkills script-supervisor practice: break down story days, action, costume and props; track screen direction, eyelines, position, dialogue and emotional continuity; keep written and photographic records.
- Sources: https://www.screenskills.com/job-profiles/browse/film-and-tv-drama/technical/script-supervisor-film-and-tv-drama/ and https://www.screenskills.com/skills-checklists/scripted-film-and-tv/script-supervisor-department/script-supervisor-skills/

## Shot joining

- Continuity editing and match-on-action: a cut inside one action must preserve action phase, direction, space and visual logic.
- Source: https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud/video/production/cinematography/camera-shots-and-angles/sequence-shot.html

## Asset references

- OpenAssetIO: separate stable entity identity from storage paths and versions; downstream consumers resolve approved canonical references instead of arbitrary filenames.
- Source: https://docs.openassetio.org/OpenAssetIO/
